I'm Riley Smith and i'm currently studying entrepreneurial
management at Brigham Young University. I've always loved
the automotive industry but the racing in particular is what is
most exciting to me. When I was 18 I bought the Mustang
pictured to the left and I started modifying it to race. After finding
much succes in going fast and hearing multiple people take
interest in the parts on my car, which I designed and built
myself, I decided to open up RS Performance and mass
produce the parts that have made me so successful. I still
own the car today and the items you see on this website are
parts that I still use. I hate doing things twice so we've done
our best to develop high quality speed parts that will last so
